Let $$u_t = (\alpha(x,t)u_x)_x + c(x,t)u_x + q(x,t)u ,\mbox{ for $x\in(\ell_1,\ell_2)$, $t>0$}. $$

By Exercise 4.20, we know that $$ (\alpha(x,t)u_x)_x = \frac{\alpha_{j+1/2}^{m}(v_{j+1}^m-v_{j}^m)-\alpha_{j-1/2}^{m}(v_{j}^m-v_{j-1}^m)}{\Delta x^2} $$ where $\alpha_{j+1/2}^{m}=(\alpha(v_{j+1}^{m})+\alpha(v_{j}^{m}))/2.$

By C.F.D, we know that $$ c(x,t)u_x=c_j\frac{v_{j+1}-v_{j-1}}{2\Delta x}.$$

Then, $$(v_t)_j =\frac{\alpha_{j+1/2}(v_{j+1}-v_{j})-\alpha_{j-1/2}(v_{j}-v_{j-1})}{\Delta x^2} + c_j\frac{v_{j+1}-v_{j-1}}{2\Delta x} + q(x,t)u\\ (v_t)_j =v_{j+1}\frac{\alpha_{j+1/2}}{\Delta x^2} -v_{j}(\frac{-\alpha_{j-1/2}}{\Delta x^2}+\frac{-\alpha_{j+1/2}}{\Delta x^2})+ v_{j-1}\frac{\alpha_{j-1/2}}{\Delta x^2} + c_j\frac{v_{j+1}-v_{j-1}}{2\Delta x} + q(x,t)v_j\\ $$

Let $$ u_t = (\alpha(u)u_x)_x ,\mbox{ for $x\in(0,1)$, $t>0$} $$ By part (a) we know that: $$v_x(x,t) \approx \frac{v(x+\Delta x/2,t)-v(x-\Delta x/2,t)}{\Delta x}$$ By part (b) we know that: $$ v(x+\Delta x/2,t) \approx \frac{1}{2}(\alpha(u(x+\Delta x,t))+\alpha(u(x,t)))\frac{u(x+\Delta x,t)-u(x,t)}{\Delta x} $$ Then, $$v_x(x,t) \approx \frac{\frac{1}{2}(\alpha(u(x+\Delta x,t))+\alpha(u(x,t)))\frac{u(x+\Delta x,t)-u(x,t)}{\Delta x}-\frac{1}{2}(\alpha(u(x-\Delta x,t))+\alpha(u(x,t)))\frac{u(x-\Delta x,t)-u(x,t)}{\Delta x}}{\Delta x}\\ v_{x} = \frac{ \alpha_{j+1/2}(u(x+\Delta x,t) -u(x,t)- \alpha_{j-1/2}(u(x,t) - u(x-\Delta x,t)}{\Delta x^2}\\ \frac{v_j^{m+1}-v_j^m}{\Delta t} = \frac{\alpha_{j+1/2}^{m}(v_{j+1}^m-v_{j}^m)-\alpha_{j-1/2}^{m}(v_{j}^m-v_{j-1}^m)}{\Delta x^2} $$ where $\alpha_{j+1/2}^{m}=(\alpha(v_{j+1}^{m})+\alpha(v_{j}^{m}))/2$
